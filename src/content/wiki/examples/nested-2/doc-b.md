## Full Job Description (Canonical Copy)

### Role Overview
BrainRocket is a global company creating end-to-end tech products across Fintech, iGaming, and Marketing. The company operates internationally and is expanding its Information Security team. This role is office-based across multiple European locations, with relocation support available.

### Key Responsibilities
- Monitor security alerts and events using SIEM, IDS/IPS, firewalls, and related tools  
- Perform initial triage and analysis of security events  
- Investigate network traffic and system logs for suspicious or malicious activity  
- Respond to and manage security incidents in a timely manner  
- Escalate incidents and coordinate response with other teams  
- Document findings, actions taken, and outcomes clearly  
- Maintain detailed records of incidents and response activities  
- Contribute to incident reports and SOC documentation  
- Participate in post-incident reviews and improve SOC playbooks and procedures  

### Tools & Platforms Referenced
- SIEM tools (Splunk, Sentinel, QRadar, ELK)  
- IDS/IPS  
- EDR solutions  
- Firewalls  
- Email and web security solutions  
- Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)  

### Scope & Seniority Notes
- Minimum 3 years experience in security operations, incident response, or threat monitoring  
- Focus on monitoring, investigation, escalation, and documentation  
- Emphasis on operational execution rather than architecture or design
